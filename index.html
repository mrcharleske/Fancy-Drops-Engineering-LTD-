About Fancy Drops Water Engineering Ltd

With over 15 years of experience in the water engineering industry, Fancy Drops Water Engineering Ltd has established itself as a leading provider of comprehensive water solutions in Kenya and East Africa.

Based in Kapenguria, we serve clients across multiple counties, delivering professional water engineering services that meet international standards. Our team of certified engineers and technicians is committed to providing sustainable water solutions for communities, businesses, and institutions.
