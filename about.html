Our Story

Founded in 2009 in Kapenguria, Kenya, Fancy Drops Water Engineering Ltd began with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide reliable, sustainable water solutions to communities and businesses across East Africa.

What started as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into Kenya's leading water engineering company, serving over 1,000 satisfied clients across 24 counties with cutting-edge technology and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, we're proud to be ISO certified, employing the latest drilling techniques, solar technology, and water treatment systems to deliver world-class solutions that transform lives and communities.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our growth from a local startup to Kenya's premier water engineering company.

Company Founded

2009

Fancy Drops Water Engineering Ltd was established in Kapenguria with a team of 3 engineers and a vision to revolutionize water access in rural Kenya.

First 100 Boreholes

2012

Reached the milestone of 100 successful borehole installations, serving over 50,000 people across West Pokot and Trans-Nzoia counties.

Solar Technology Integration

2016

Pioneered solar-powered water systems in Kenya, becoming the first company in the region to offer comprehensive solar water solutions.

ISO Certification

2019

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification for quality management systems, reinforcing our commitment to international standards.

Regional Expansion

2022

Expanded operations to serve all 47 counties in Kenya and began projects in Uganda and Tanzania, establishing our East African presence.

1000+ Projects Milestone

2024

Celebrating over 1,000 successful projects and continuing to lead innovation in sustainable water engineering across East Africa.
