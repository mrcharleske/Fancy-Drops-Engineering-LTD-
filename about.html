Cornelius Kitayi

Technical Project Manager

Cornelius Kitayi is a dedicated WASH (Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene) professional with over 12 years of progressive experience in water engineering, project management, and sustainable infrastructure development.

He holds a Bachelor's degree in Project Planning and Management from the University of Nairobi and a Diploma in Water Engineering from the Kenya Water Institute.

Cornelius has led numerous successful water projects across Kenya and the region, ranging from solar-powered water supply systems and borehole drilling to treatment plant installations and community water solutions.

As Managing Director, he oversees company operations, project execution, and stakeholder engagement, ensuring that every project meets the highest standards of quality and sustainability. His leadership is guided by a deep passion for innovation and a commitment to expanding access to clean water.

Core Strengths:

  • Water system design and implementation
  • Solar water pumping and treatment technologies
  • Project planning and execution
  • Stakeholder coordination and capacity building
  • Sustainable and community-centered water solutions

Alfred Ndiema

Water Engineer

Alfred Ndiema is a highly skilled Water Engineer with extensive expertise in designing and implementing comprehensive water solutions across diverse environments.

With a strong background in hydrogeological surveys, borehole design, and water treatment systems, Alfred ensures every project is built on solid technical foundations and delivers sustainable results.

Alfred is passionate about leveraging technology and innovation to overcome water scarcity challenges, and he works closely with clients to develop customized solutions that meet their specific needs.

Core Strengths:
