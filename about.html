Our Story

Building a Legacy of Water Excellence

Founded with a vision to transform water access across Kenya, Fancy Drops Water Engineering Limited has grown into a trusted leader in comprehensive water solutions. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to deliver reliable, sustainable, and affordable water systems to communities, farms, and businesses.

Over the years, we've drilled hundreds of boreholes, installed cutting-edge water treatment systems, and pioneered solar-powered water solutions that reduce costs while protecting the environment. Every project we undertake is guided by our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we stand proud as one of Kenya's most respected water engineering firms, serving clients across Kapenguria, West Pokot, and beyond with unmatched expertise and dedication.

About Cornelius

Cornelius Kitayi is a dedicated WASH (Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene) professional with more than 12 years of progressive experience in water engineering, project management, and sustainable infrastructure development. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Project Planning and Management from the University of Nairobi and a Diploma in Water Engineering from the Kenya Water Institute.

Cornelius has led numerous successful water projects across Kenya and the region, ranging from solar-powered water supply systems and borehole drilling to treatment plant installations and community water solutions. His technical expertise, strategic planning skills, and hands-on leadership have positioned Fancy Drops Water Engineering Ltd as a trusted partner in delivering reliable, safe, and affordable water solutions.

As Managing Director, Cornelius oversees company operations, project execution, and stakeholder engagement, ensuring that every project meets the highest standards of quality and sustainability. His leadership is guided by a deep passion for innovation and a commitment to expanding access to clean water for communities, institutions, and industries.

Core Strengths:

  • Solar water pumping and treatment technologies
  • Project planning and execution
  • Stakeholder coordination and capacity building
